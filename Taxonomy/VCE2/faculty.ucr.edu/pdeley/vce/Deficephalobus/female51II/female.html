Adult Female of Deficephalobus humophilus (Zell, 1987) De Ley &Coomans, 1990

Virtual specimen prepared by Luis Mundo, Melissa Yoder and Paul De Ley

cf. ZELL, H.  (1987). Nematoden eines Buchenwaldbodens: 9. Die Cephaloben (Nematoda, Rhabditida). Carolinea, 45: 121-134.

cf. DE LEY, P. & COOMANS, A. (1990). Terrestrial Nematodes of the Galápagos Archipelago I: Three Rhabditida from Isla Fernandina. Bulletin van het Koninklijk Belgisch Instituut voor Natuurwetenschappen - Biologie, 60: 5-22.

Status: No type status, not collected at or near type locality.

Source: Soil from a chicken coop in back yard of private residence in Merelbeke, East Flanders, Belgium. Collected by A. De Grisse (date unknown - presumably early 80Ís).
