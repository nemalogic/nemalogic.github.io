Adult Female of Cervidellus vexilliger (de Man, 1880) Thorne, 1937

Virtual specimen prepared by Melissa Yoder, Luis Mundo, and Paul De Ley

cf. BOSTRÖM, S. & DE LEY, P. (1996). Redescription of Cervidellus vexilliger (de Man, 1880) Thorne, 1937 (Nematoda: Cephalobidae) and taxonomical implications. Fundamental and Applied Nematology, 19: 329-340.

Specimen status: No type status, not from type locality assigned by Boström & De Ley (1996). However, this specimen was collected from Wernhout, one of the localities from which de Man reported C. vexilliger in his later publication of 1921. Kept in slide with Accession No. ƒ of the UCR Nematode Collection.

Remark: Note the very prominent spermatheca and open phasmids.
