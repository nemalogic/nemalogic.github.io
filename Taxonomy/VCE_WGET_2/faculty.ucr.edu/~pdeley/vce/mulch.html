Video Capture and Editing of heat-killed nematodes observed without coverslip

Two examples of partially captured nematodes from a mulch sample

Both nematodes are from a quick screening of one year old mulch, collected by Mark Hoddle in an avocado orchard in Temecula, CA.

Both nematodes have lip regions and tails that would be impossible to represent with a single still image.

Achromadora sp.

  • Entire nematode at 10x
  • Lip region at 100x
  • Pharyngeal region at 20x1.6x
  • Basal bulb at 100x
  • Vulva and part of posterior gonad at 100x
  • Tail at 20x
  • Tail tip at 100x1x

Mononchoides sp.

  • Entire nematode at 10x
  • Lip region at 100x1.25x
  • Pharyngeal region at 20x1.6x
  • Spicules at 100x1.25x
  • Tail at 20x1.25x
