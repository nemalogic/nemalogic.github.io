Adult Female of Odontopharynx longicaudata de Man, 1912

Virtual specimen prepared by Melissa Yoder, Sheila Esfhani, and Paul De Ley

Status: No type status, not collected at or near type locality.

Source: This specimen was collected by A. Coomans in 1993 from the Victoria pond in the tropical greenhouse of Ghent University Botanical Garden.

Remark: Note dorsal gland canal running through dorsal tooth and opening below its tip.
